# 5-Minute Execution Playbook (Version 2.0 — Bullish & Bearish)

**Purpose:** Execute trades only after price reaches a Higher Timeframe (15M) POI — works for both long (bullish) and short (bearish) setups.

> **Golden Rule:** Location tells me **WHERE** to trade. Order Flow tells me **WHEN** to trade.

---

## Phase 0 — Before Price Reaches POI
- Determine 15M bias (bullish or bearish).
- Mark only HTF POIs:
  - **Bullish bias →** Demand zones / bullish OB / discount arrays.
  - **Bearish bias →** Supply zones / bearish OB / premium arrays.
- Do not mark every BOS/CHoCH.
- Wait.

## Phase 1 — Price Reaches HTF POI
A POI is an **Area of Interest**, not an entry — whether it's a demand zone (bullish) or supply zone (bearish).

## Phase 2 — Reaction
Wait for evidence:

| Bullish (at demand) | Bearish (at supply) |
|---|---|
| Rejection (wicks down, closes up) | Rejection (wicks up, closes down) |
| Displacement up | Displacement down |
| Strong buying | Strong selling |

If absent: **WAIT.**

## Phase 3 — Displacement
Confirm:
- Large displacement (in direction of intended trade)
- FVG/imbalance
- BOS (bullish BOS for longs, bearish BOS for shorts)

## Phase 4 — BOS
Only use BOS that occurs **inside your POI**.
- Bullish: higher-low structure breaks a prior high.
- Bearish: lower-high structure breaks a prior low.

## Phase 5 — Retracement
**Good retracement** (either direction):
- Slow
- Overlapping
- Small candles
- Wicks

**Bad retracement:**
- Large candles against your bias (large bearish candles into a bullish retracement, or large bullish candles into a bearish retracement)
- Fast momentum against your bias

## Phase 6 — Failure of the Opposite Side
Ask:

| Bullish setup — sellers should fail to: | Bearish setup — buyers should fail to: |
|---|---|
| Close below displacement low | Close above displacement high |
| Break the POI | Break the POI |
| Make a Lower Low (LL) | Make a Higher High (HH) |

## Phase 7 — Entry Trigger
Enter only after:

**Bullish:**
- Bullish engulfing, or
- Fresh bullish displacement, or
- Bullish BOS after retracement.

**Bearish:**
- Bearish engulfing, or
- Fresh bearish displacement, or
- Bearish BOS after retracement.

## Phase 8 — Stop Loss
Place where your idea is invalid:

| Bullish | Bearish |
|---|---|
| Below retracement low | Above retracement high |
| Below bullish OB | Above bearish OB |
| Below sweep (aggressive) | Above sweep (aggressive) |

## Phase 9 — Targets

| Bullish | Bearish |
|---|---|
| Buy-side liquidity | Sell-side liquidity |
| HTF swing high | HTF swing low |
| Next HTF supply | Next HTF demand |

---

## Rules
1. POI ≠ Entry.
2. Sweep ≠ Reversal.
3. BOS outside POI ≠ Execution signal.
4. Don't chase displacement.
5. Missing a trade is better than forcing one.
6. Bias determines direction — never take a bullish entry pattern at a bearish POI, or vice versa.

## Mantra
> I don't trade because price reached my zone. I trade because price reacted to my zone — in the direction my bias expects.
