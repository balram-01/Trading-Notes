# 📘 5-Minute Execution Playbook — v3.0
### *The Only Setup for My First 100 Trades*

---

## 🔗 The Setup (Multi-Timeframe Flow)

```
┌─────────────────────┐
│      4H HTF POI      │   ← Bias + Zone (Demand or Supply)
└──────────┬───────────┘
           ↓
┌─────────────────────┐
│    15M Reaction       │   ← Price arrives & reacts at the 4H zone
└──────────┬───────────┘
           ↓
┌─────────────────────┐
│    Displacement       │   ← Strong, aggressive move off the zone
└──────────┬───────────┘
           ↓
┌─────────────────────┐
│      MSS / BOS         │   ← Structure shift confirms new direction
└──────────┬───────────┘
           ↓
┌─────────────────────┐
│ Execution OB/FVG Forms │  ← The tradeable zone is created
└──────────┬───────────┘
           ↓
┌─────────────────────┐
│   5M Retracement       │  ← Price pulls back into the OB/FVG
└──────────┬───────────┘
           ↓
┌─────────────────────┐
│        ENTRY           │
└─────────────────────┘
```

> **Golden Rule:** The **4H** tells me *where*. The **15M** tells me price is *reacting*. The **5M** tells me *when* to pull the trigger.

## 🎯 Bias Reference

| | Bullish | Bearish |
|---|---|---|
| **4H POI** | Demand zone / bullish OB / discount array | Supply zone / bearish OB / premium array |
| **Displacement** | Strong impulsive move **up** | Strong impulsive move **down** |
| **MSS/BOS** | Break of prior high (shift bullish) | Break of prior low (shift bearish) |
| **Execution zone** | Bullish OB / FVG left behind | Bearish OB / FVG left behind |
| **Entry trigger** | Bullish engulfing / fresh bullish displacement / bullish BOS after retrace | Bearish engulfing / fresh bearish displacement / bearish BOS after retrace |

## 🪜 Phase-by-Phase

### Phase 0 — 4H Bias & POI
- Determine 4H bias.
- Mark **only** the 4H HTF POI (demand for bullish, supply for bearish).
- Don't mark every BOS/CHoCH — one zone, one bias.
- **Wait** for price to travel to it.

### Phase 1 — Arrival at POI
The 4H POI is an **Area of Interest**, not an entry.

### Phase 2 — 15M Reaction
Look for evidence at the zone:

| Bullish | Bearish |
|---|---|
| Rejection wicks down, closes up | Rejection wicks up, closes down |
| Buying pressure | Selling pressure |

If absent → **WAIT.**

### Phase 3 — Displacement
Confirm on the 15M:
- Large, aggressive candle(s) in the bias direction
- FVG / imbalance left behind
- Clean, decisive move (not choppy)

### Phase 4 — MSS / BOS
- Must occur **inside** the 4H POI.
- Bullish: break of prior 15M high.
- Bearish: break of prior 15M low.
- This confirms the shift — it's your permission to drop to the 5M.

### Phase 5 — Execution OB/FVG Forms
The displacement + BOS leaves behind a **lower-timeframe OB or FVG** — this becomes your execution zone on the 5M.

### Phase 6 — 5M Retracement
**Good retracement:**
- Slow, overlapping candles
- Small bodies, visible wicks
- Price gently drifting back into the OB/FVG

**Bad retracement:**
- Large candles against your bias
- Fast, aggressive momentum tearing back through the zone

### Phase 7 — Failure of the Opposite Side
Ask, on the 5M:

| Bullish setup — sellers should fail to: | Bearish setup — buyers should fail to: |
|---|---|
| Close below the execution zone low | Close above the execution zone high |
| Break the 4H POI | Break the 4H POI |
| Print a Lower Low | Print a Higher High |

### Phase 8 — Entry Trigger
Enter only after, on the 5M:
- Engulfing candle in bias direction, **or**
- Fresh displacement in bias direction, **or**
- BOS in bias direction after the retracement.

### Phase 9 — Stop Loss

| Bullish | Bearish |
|---|---|
| Below retracement low | Above retracement high |
| Below the 5M/execution OB | Above the 5M/execution OB |
| Below the sweep (aggressive) | Above the sweep (aggressive) |

### Phase 10 — Targets

| Bullish | Bearish |
|---|---|
| Buy-side liquidity | Sell-side liquidity |
| 4H/15M swing high | 4H/15M swing low |
| Next 4H supply | Next 4H demand |

## ✅ Rules
1. **4H POI ≠ Entry.**
2. **Sweep ≠ Reversal.**
3. **MSS/BOS outside the 4H POI ≠ Execution signal.**
4. **Don't chase displacement** — wait for the OB/FVG and the retracement.
5. **Missing a trade is better than forcing one.**
6. **One direction per setup** — never mix a bullish trigger with a bearish POI, or vice versa.
7. **This is the only setup for the first 100 trades.** No other models, no exceptions.

## 🧭 Mantra
> I don't trade because price reached my zone. I trade because price displaced, shifted structure, and gave me back a clean retracement into my execution zone — in the direction my 4H bias expects.
