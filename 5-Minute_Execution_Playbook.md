# 5-Minute Execution Playbook (Version 1.0)

**Purpose:** Execute trades only after price reaches a Higher Timeframe
(15M) POI.

> **Golden Rule:** Location tells me **WHERE** to trade. Order Flow
> tells me **WHEN** to trade.

## Phase 0 --- Before Price Reaches POI

-   Determine 15M bias.
-   Mark only HTF POIs.
-   Do not mark every BOS/CHoCH.
-   Wait.

## Phase 1 --- Price Reaches HTF POI

A POI is an **Area of Interest**, not an entry.

## Phase 2 --- Reaction

Wait for evidence: - Rejection - Displacement - Strong selling

If absent: WAIT.

## Phase 3 --- Displacement

Confirm: - Large displacement - FVG/imbalance - BOS

## Phase 4 --- BOS

Only use BOS that occurs **inside your POI**.

## Phase 5 --- Retracement

Good retracement: - Slow - Overlapping - Small candles - Wicks

Bad retracement: - Large bullish candles - Fast momentum

## Phase 6 --- Buyer Failure

Ask: - Failed to close above displacement high? - Failed to break POI? -
Failed to make HH?

## Phase 7 --- Entry Trigger

Enter only after: - Bearish engulfing, or - Fresh bearish displacement,
or - Bearish BOS after retracement.

## Phase 8 --- Stop Loss

Place where your idea is invalid: - Above retracement high - Above
bearish OB - Above sweep (aggressive)

## Phase 9 --- Targets

-   Sell-side liquidity
-   HTF swing low
-   Next HTF demand

## Rules

1.  POI ≠ Entry.
2.  Sweep ≠ Reversal.
3.  BOS outside POI ≠ Execution signal.
4.  Don't chase displacement.
5.  Missing a trade is better than forcing one.

## Mantra

> I don't trade because price reached my zone. I trade because price
> reacted to my zone.
