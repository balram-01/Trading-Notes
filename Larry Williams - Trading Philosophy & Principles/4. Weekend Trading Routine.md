# 🗓 Weekend Trading Routine

Every Saturday:

## 1. Scan Every Market

Look for markets likely to:

- Rally
- Decline

Use:

- Market cycles
- Seasonality
- COT report
- Commercial accumulation

---

## 2. Check Daily Charts

Determine:

- Current trend
- Trend strength
- Potential trend reversals

---

## 3. Build a Watchlist

Only keep markets with the strongest conditions.

Quality over quantity.

---

## 4. Wait Patiently

Do not force trades.

Only participate when:

- Market conditions align
- Technical setup confirms
- Risk is acceptable
