# 🧘 Trading Psychology

---

## Emotional Stability

Great traders remain emotionally balanced.

Avoid becoming:

- Overexcited after wins
- Devastated after losses

Every trade is simply:

> One outcome within a long series of trades.

---

## Characteristics of Successful Traders

Successful traders are often:

- Humble
- Detail-oriented
- Disciplined
- Patient
- Internally motivated
- Comfortable under stress

Confidence comes from preparation—not ego.

---

## Imposter Syndrome

Many consistently profitable traders experience thoughts like:

- "Maybe I'm just lucky."
- "What if this stops working?"

This is common.

Long-term success does not eliminate self-doubt.

Discipline matters more than confidence.
