# 🛡 Risk Management

---

## The 4% Rule

A practical balance:

**Risk approximately 4% of account capital per trade.**

Example:

- 1 Loss → -4%
- 2 Losses → -8%
- 3 Losses → -12%

A manageable drawdown while still allowing meaningful account growth.

---

## Risk vs Reward

Higher returns require accepting larger risk.

However:

Higher risk without high accuracy quickly destroys accounts.

Professional traders focus on:

- Controlled risk
- Consistent execution
- Long-term survival

---

## Capital Preservation First

The first objective is **not making money.**

The first objective is:

> Stay in the game.

Without capital:

There is no next trade.
