# Guiding Principles & Creed

## ⭐ Guiding Principles

- Markets are driven by conditions, not predictions.
- Technical analysis provides timing—not certainty.
- Simplicity beats unnecessary complexity.
- Capital preservation comes before profit.
- Patience creates opportunity.
- Trends pay.
- Risk management keeps you alive.
- Discipline beats intelligence.
- Observation beats prediction.
- Consistency beats excitement.

---

## 📜 Final Trading Creed

> **I do not predict markets.**
>
> I observe conditions.
>
> I follow the trend.
>
> I manage risk.
>
> I remain emotionally neutral.
>
> I protect my capital.
>
> I execute my plan with discipline.
>
> **The market owes me nothing, but consistency will reward me over time.**
