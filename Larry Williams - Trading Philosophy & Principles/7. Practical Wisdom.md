# ⚠ Practical Wisdom

---

## Avoid Blowups

Most traders fail because they:

- Chase unrealistic returns
- Buy "holy grail" systems
- Believe flashy marketing
- Ignore risk management

Protecting capital is more important than maximizing returns.

---

## Don't Overthink

High intelligence can become a disadvantage.

Over-analysis often creates:

- Hesitation
- Missed trades
- Emotional decisions

Simple observation usually outperforms complex prediction.

---

## Find The Right Mentor

Choose a mentor whose concepts:

- Make immediate sense
- Match your thinking style
- Can be applied consistently

If you're constantly confused,

it's probably not the right teacher.

---

## Trade With The Horse

> It is easier to ride the horse in the direction it is already running.

Trade with:

- Trend
- Momentum
- Market conditions

Don't fight the market simply because a reversal seems "due."

---

## Effort Determines Results

Trading rewards:

- Practice
- Observation
- Discipline
- Continuous learning

There are no shortcuts.

> **You only get out of trading what you put into it.**
