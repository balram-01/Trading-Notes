# 📈 Open Interest

## Definition

Open Interest represents:

> The total number of open futures contracts currently held by market participants.

---

## High Open Interest

Extremely high Open Interest often appears near:

- Significant tops
- Significant bottoms

Reason:

Most traders are already committed.

If everyone is already buying…

Who is left to buy?

---

## Interpretation Matters

Don't just observe Open Interest.

Determine **who created it**.

### If driven by Commercials

✅ Often bullish

Commercials typically hedge near value.

---

### If driven by Small Speculators

⚠️ Often bearish

Retail traders frequently become heavily positioned near market extremes.
