# Commitment of Traders (COT) Report

## What Are Commercials?

Commercial traders are:

- Producers
- Manufacturers
- Users of commodities
- Hedgers

They trade for business purposes—not speculation.

---

## Accumulation

Commercials frequently accumulate positions during periods of market weakness.

This **does NOT** mean the market must immediately rally.

Instead it suggests:

- Undervaluation
- Supply imbalance
- Long-term opportunity

Think of accumulation as:

> **A condition—not a timing signal.**

---

## How To Use COT

The COT report is a **suggestive indicator**, not a trading signal.

Use it to identify markets that are:

- Extremely bullish
- Extremely bearish

Then combine it with:

- Technical Analysis
- Trend analysis
- Price action

Timing always comes from price.
