# 🪞 Trading Reflection & Review Notes

> *"The market is a mirror. It doesn't create your weaknesses—it exposes them."*

---

# 🎯 Purpose of Reflection

Reflection is one of the highest ROI activities in trading.

Most traders believe improvement comes from:
- Taking more trades
- Learning more strategies
- Backtesting endlessly

Professional traders improve by reviewing their execution.

Every review helps you:
- Improve your trading plan
- Identify recurring mistakes
- Strengthen discipline
- Build consistency

> **Rule:** Don't just trade. Review your trading.

---

# 📈 The Reflection Cycle

```text
Trade
      ↓
Journal
      ↓
Review
      ↓
Identify Mistakes
      ↓
Improve Rules
      ↓
Better Execution
      ↓
Repeat
```

Over time, this creates continuous improvement.

# 📅 End-of-Day Review Checklist

## 1️⃣ Review Daily Statistics

Start by reviewing the overall trading day.

### Check:

- ✅ Net Profit/Loss
- ✅ Win Rate
- ✅ Average R Multiple
- ✅ Number of Trades
- ✅ Trading Hours
- ✅ Guardrail Violations

---

## 2️⃣ Review Process Metrics

Ask yourself:

- Did I complete my pre-market routine?
- Did I follow my trading plan?
- Did I execute according to my rules?
- Did I journal every trade?
- Did I stay disciplined?

Remember:

> **A profitable day with poor discipline is still a bad day.**

---

# 🧠 Four Reflection Questions

Ask these questions every single day.

### 1. Did I follow my trading plan?

OR

Did I trade based on emotions?

---

### 2. Did I break any rules?

Examples:

- Overtrading
- FOMO entry
- Moving stop loss
- Entering outside Killzone
- Trading during news

---

### 3. Was my execution clean?

Consider:

- Entry quality
- Exit quality
- Patience
- Timing
- Risk management

---

### 4. Did I complete the boring work?

Professionals never skip:

- Chart markups
- Pre-market routine
- Journaling
- Reflection

---

# 📊 Individual Trade Review

Don't only review the day.

Review every trade individually.

Focus on:

- Best Trade
- Worst Trade

---

# 🏆 Best Trade Analysis

Study your winners.

Ask:

### What created this win?

Examples:

- Daily bias aligned
- Liquidity sweep
- Market shift
- Quality POI
- Good patience

---

### What did I do correctly?

Examples:

- Waited patiently
- Followed checklist
- Proper stop placement
- Correct target
- Controlled emotions

---

### How can I repeat this tomorrow?

Your goal is to repeat good behavior.

Not chase profits.

---

# ❌ Worst Trade Analysis

Study your mistakes.

Ask:

### What caused this trade?

Be specific.

Examples:

- FOMO
- Early entry
- Wrong bias
- Ignored liquidity
- No confirmation
- Trading during news

---

### Was this a Good Loss or Bad Loss?

### ✅ Good Loss

- Followed plan
- Correct setup
- Market simply didn't work

Accept it.

This is part of probabilities.

---

### ❌ Bad Loss

- Broke rules
- Emotional decision
- Poor discipline

These losses are preventable.

---

### What is ONE improvement for tomorrow?

Never write:

> "I'll do better."

Instead write:

- Wait for liquidity sweep.
- Stop entering early.
- Reduce trades to two.
- Respect stop loss.
- Trade only London session.

Small improvements compound.

---

# 🚧 Guardrail Violations

Whenever you break a rule, document:

### What rule?

Example:

- Took 5 trades instead of 2

---

### Why?

Example:

- Revenge trading
- Greed
- Boredom
- Fear

---

### How will I prevent it?

Example:

- Close TradingView after second trade.
- Remove Buy/Sell button after max loss.
- Set platform alerts only.

---

# ⚠️ Dangerous Scenario

One of the most dangerous situations is:

> **Breaking rules and still making money.**

Why?

Because your brain learns:

> "Breaking rules works."

This creates long-term bad habits.

Never reward bad behavior.

Only reward proper execution.

---

# 🧘 Mental Reset (Sanctuary)

Spend time improving your mind.

A good rule:

> **1 hour of inner work for every 1 hour of chart work.**

Ideas:

- Meditation
- Box Breathing
- Walking
- Prayer
- Deep Breathing
- Journaling
- Visualization

Purpose:

- Reset emotions
- Remove greed
- Remove fear
- Detach from today's P&L
- Prepare for tomorrow

Never carry today's emotions into tomorrow.

---

# 🤖 Using AI for Reflection

After trading, ask AI:

### Summarize today's trading.

Include:

- Total Profit/Loss
- Win Rate
- Biggest Mistake
- Best Decision
- Dominant Emotion

---

Then ask:

> **"What is ONE improvement I should focus on tomorrow?"**

Focus on only one improvement at a time.
