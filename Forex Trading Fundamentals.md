# 🌍 What is Forex?

**Forex (Foreign Exchange)** is the global marketplace where currencies are exchanged at agreed exchange rates.

### 📌 Key Facts

- 🌎 Largest financial market in the world.
    
- 💰 Daily trading volume: **~$6.6 Trillion**
    
- 🕒 Open **24 hours a day**, **5 days a week** _(Monday–Friday)_
    

---

# 💱 Understanding Currency Pairs

Forex is always traded in **pairs**.

Example:

```text
EUR/USD
```

- **EUR** → Base Currency (Always equals **1 unit**)
    
- **USD** → Quote Currency
    

The displayed price tells you **how much of the quote currency is required to buy 1 unit of the base currency.**

### 📈 Buying (Long)

You expect:

- Base currency ⬆️
    
- Quote currency ⬇️
    

### 📉 Selling (Short)

You expect:

- Base currency ⬇️
    
- Quote currency ⬆️
    

---

# 🌐 Types of Currency Pairs

|Type|Description|Examples|
|---|---|---|
|🌟 **Major Pairs**|Include the US Dollar (USD).|EUR/USD, GBP/USD|
|🔄 **Minor Pairs**|Two major currencies without USD.|EUR/JPY, EUR/CHF|
|🌍 **Exotic Pairs**|One major currency paired with an emerging market currency.|USD/TRY, USD/ZAR|

# 🎯 The 3 M's of Profitable Trading

Success in trading depends on three essential pillars:

## 📊 1. Method

A trading strategy that remains effective across different market conditions.

---

## 💰 2. Money Management

Focus on:

- Consistent risk per trade.
    
- Favorable Risk-to-Reward ratios.
    
- Ensuring winning trades outweigh losing trades over time.
    

---

## 🧠 3. Mindset

The discipline to:

- Follow your trading plan.
    
- Execute trades mechanically.
    
- Avoid emotional decision-making.
    

---

# 📖 Essential Forex Terminology

|Term|Meaning|
|---|---|
|**Pip**|The smallest standard price movement _(usually the 4th decimal place; 2nd decimal for JPY pairs)._|
|**Pipette**|One-tenth of a pip _(5th decimal place)._|
|**Lot Size**|The number of currency units traded.|
|**Bullish**|Expecting price to rise.|
|**Bearish**|Expecting price to fall.|
|**Stop Loss (SL)**|Automatically closes a losing trade at a predefined level.|
|**Take Profit (TP)**|Automatically closes a winning trade at a predefined target.|

---

## 📦 Lot Sizes

|Lot Type|Units|Approx. Value per Pip|
|---|--:|--:|
|Standard|100,000|$10|
|Mini|10,000|$1|
|Micro|1,000|$0.10|

---

# 🧮 Profit & Loss Calculation

### Formula

```text
Profit / Loss = Pip Movement × Dollars per Pip
```

# 💵 Trading Costs

### 📌 Bid / Ask Spread

The difference between:

- **Bid** → Price at which you sell.
    
- **Ask** → Price at which you buy.
    

---

### 💳 Commission

A fixed fee charged by your broker for each trade.

---

### 🌙 Swap (Rollover)

A fee paid—or interest earned—for holding a position overnight.

---

# ⚠️ Best Practices

Avoid these common mistakes:

- ❌ Overcomplicating your charts with too many indicators.
    
- ❌ Trading all day, which can lead to fatigue and overtrading.
    
- ❌ Connecting your brokerage account directly to TradingView, reducing the risk of accidental or emotional trades.
    
- ❌ Choosing a broker solely based on recommendations without conducting your own research.

# ⭐ Key Takeaways

- Forex is the world's largest financial market.
