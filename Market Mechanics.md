# 📘 Lesson 1 — Market Mechanics: Core Framework

---

## 🎯 Objective

Understand **how the market actually moves** by focusing on institutional concepts instead of relying on traditional technical analysis alone.

---

# 🧠 What is Market Mechanics?

**Definition**

Market Mechanics is a trading framework that aims to uncover the **hidden logic behind price movement**.

Instead of depending on:

- ❌ Chart patterns
    
- ❌ Indicators
    
- ❌ Memorized candlestick patterns
    

It focuses on understanding **why price moves**, not just **how it looks**.

---

# 🏗️ Foundation of the Framework

The strategy is built around six core concepts:

|Concept|Purpose|
|---|---|
|**Structural Liquidity**|Find areas where traders' stop-losses are likely placed.|
|**Imbalance**|Identify inefficient price movements that may later be revisited.|
|**Institutional Zones**|Locate areas where large market participants may enter or exit positions.|
|**Multi-Timeframe Analysis**|Use higher timeframes for context and lower timeframes for execution.|
|**Timing**|Trade during sessions that provide the best opportunities.|
|**Execution & Psychology**|Execute trades with discipline while controlling emotions.|

---

# 🔬 How the Strategy Was Built

The instructor's development process:

1. **Test** → Backtest ideas across **100–500+ trades**.
    
2. **Filter** → Remove concepts that don't consistently work.
    
3. **Refine** → Keep only the concepts supported by data and experience.
    
4. **Build a Playbook** → Create a repeatable trading system.
    

> 💡 **Instructor's View:** Around **70%** of commonly taught trading concepts are unnecessary. Focus on the **30%** that consistently produce results.

# 🧠 Trading Mindset

### 🪞 The Mirror Principle

> **"Your P&L is a mirror of your character."**

Your trading results reflect:

- Discipline
    
- Habits
    
- Emotional control
    
- Decision-making
    

Improving yourself improves your trading.

---

### 🌊 Abundance Mentality

- Don't chase missed trades.
    
- The market always provides new opportunities.
    
- Wait patiently for price to reach your planned area.
    

---

### 💼 Professional Conduct

A professional trader:

- ✅ Trades with a clear plan.
    
- ✅ Accepts losses without emotional reactions.
    
- ✅ Avoids revenge trading.
